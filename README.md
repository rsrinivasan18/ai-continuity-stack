# AI Continuity Stack

Stop starting from zero every session.

Most people give their AI a resume. This repo helps you give it a relationship.

---

## The 3 layers

| File | What it carries | Who has it |
|---|---|---|
| `layer1_knowledge.md` | Your project, stack, goals | Most people |
| `layer2_session.md` | What happened last session | Some people |
| `layer3_relationship.md` | How you two work together | Almost nobody |

Layer 3 is the missing piece. Start there.

---

## How to use

1. Copy all 3 templates
2. Fill them in (takes 20-30 mins total)
3. Upload at the start of every new AI session
4. Say "time to update our relationship" when things change

Works with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, Mistral — any AI that reads a file.

---

## The trigger phrase

When your working dynamic changes — new project, new pace, something that annoyed you — just say:

> "time to update our relationship"

Update the file together. Save it. Done.
